Data Storage

Data centers provide secure and reliable storage for an organization's data, along with robust backup and recovery solutions to protect against data loss. Data centers are the foundation for cloud computing, providing the infrastructure for public, private, and hybrid cloud environments.

Networking

They offer the networking infrastructure (routers, switches, firewalls, etc.) to connect servers, storage, and other components within the data center, as well as enabling connectivity to the outside world.

Application Hosting

They host a wide range of applications, including email, CRM, ERP, e-commerce platforms, and even gaming servers. Data centers house the servers and other computing resources that power business applications.
